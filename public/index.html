Featured Publications

Fábio Nishida, Alexandre Nicolella, Bruno Canassa, Diego Espinoza, Thalles Liduares
January, 2022 Brazilian Journal of Rural Economics and Socielogy (RESR), spe
Impacts of rural pension on school dropout in Brazil

The study reveals a negative and significant impact of retirement on the dropout of boys, especially in the presence of male pensioners in the household. This effect is not observed for girls. This evidence suggests pro-male biases possibly generated by expectations related to the gender of the young, in which boys must prepare for the job market while girls contribute to household chores.
